Our Story

Brewing Success Since 2016

From a small tea stall in Indore to India's largest chai chain - a journey of passion, tradition, and innovation.

9+ Years of
Excellence

A 100-Crore Success Story Without External Investment

Chai Sutta Bar was founded in 2016 by two young and passionate entrepreneurs, Anubhav Dubey and Anand Nayak, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. What started with a humble investment of ₹2 lakhs has now grown into a 100+ crore brand.

Our vision is simple yet profound - to serve India's most loved beverage 'Chai' in the most eco-friendly way possible. By using traditional Kulhads (earthen cups), we not only preserve the authentic taste but also support local artisans and reduce plastic waste.

Despite our name, we are a smoke-free and alcohol-free establishment, creating a bar-like ambiance where youth can connect, converse, and create memories over a perfect cup of tea.
